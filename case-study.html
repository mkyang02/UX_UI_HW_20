Introduction: Alden App

The goal of this project was to create a tutor app that gives the user (tutee) power to choose what kind of tutor that wanted to work with based on their profile, skills, and style of tutoring. The idea was to create a filter search for user to further their search options.

HYPOTHESIS

Problem Statement

The Problem: How might we help students connect with online tutors to build confidence in their studies?

The Solution: We want students to get the most out of their experience when working with a tutor. We provide an app that assists with searching for a tutor based on the student's needs. There is the option to meet up in person or remotely for the subject selected. Everything is transparent before booking, by placing pricing and reviews with the tutors available.
